Jamaica

You deserve a break! The past couple of years have been rough. Between the pandemic, climate change, and now ailens? "I need a break!" Find me on an island sipping margaritas! Warm Caribbean weather with occasional liquid sunshine makes for an enjoyable holiday. sunsets in Negril so beautiful they make you fall in love, Jamaica is one of the most romantic holiday destinations in the world. Lay claim to your spot on our picturesque beaches. Whether you're interested in exploring the underwater world or soaking up the sun, there's a beach for you in Jamaica.

Maldives

A visit to the Maldives is a dream come true because of the relaxed island life.magine yourself waking up on a stunning island, with your toes in the soft sand, surrounded by swaying palm trees and azure blue sea every day. he Maldives is world-famous for its myriad of marine life, and is considered to be one of the best snorkelling and diving locations in the world. From the picturesque beaches with soft white sand, beautiful azure sea, and pristine clear skies, the term tropical paradise is almost an understatement for the Maldives.

Caymen Islands

The Cayman Islands are renowned for their pristine beaches. Lining Grand Cayman, the most famous is award winning, Seven Mile Beach.ts waters are warm year round, and you only need to dust the powder white sand off your feet before walking downtown. Start with a Rum and Beer with Lunch tour right from Seven Mile Beach, featuring varieties of local rum, beer and delicious foods. Renowned world-wide as one of the most remarkable dive destinations anywhere, there are too many sites to list.

Brazil

An eclectic mix of people, culture, and stunning landscapes, all combined into one vibrant and captivating destination. This is where the forests and rivers hide the greatest biodiversity on the planet; this is where the most lively cities will capture your heart. Most of Brazil sits south of the equator. Visiting the country is a fantastic idea no matter what the time of year as the weather is generally perfect! Brazil is the best country to travel to if you want to escape the cold of the Northern Hemisphere between November and March.

Carribean

White sandy beaches, clear pristine waters, and lush forests cover the Caribbean Islands, and many of the islands have a bunch of exploration endeavors. You can jump in the water for some snorkeling and swimming with dolphins, or opt for a relaxing sail instead. The Caribbean is a paradise getaway for travelers any time of the year. Almost every day is clear and sunny, though there is one (or two) bad storms a year. Still, the Caribbean is one of the best winter destinations as it provides a warming escape from the cold.
